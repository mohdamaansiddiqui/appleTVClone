Is Apple TV+ accessible?

Apple TV+ is part of the Apple TV app, which includes many accessibility features — so you only have to focus on enjoying Apple Original shows and movies. All Apple Originals support Closed Captions and SDH (subtitles for the Deaf and Hard of Hearing) in addition to audio descriptions; plus the entire app supports VoiceOver, Apple’s built-in screenreader. Bold Text, Increase Contrast and Reduce Motion settings are also available. Subtitles are supported for 40+ languages for all Apple TV+ titles.
